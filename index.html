Dengue Fever

Dengue fever is an infectious disease transmitted by mosquito and caused by any of the four related dengue viruses, it is also called break-bone fever because it may cause sever joint and musle pain.

    Types of Dengue Fever

  • Undifferentiated fever
  • Dengue fever (DF)
  • Dengue Haemorrhagic fever (DHF)
  • Dengue Shock Syndrome (DSS)

Symptoms of Dengue fever

After an infected mosquito's bite, incubation period of 4-7 days is required before the symptoms appear in the patient. (The symptoms can be different with the types of fever).
